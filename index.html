How long does a typical lock rekey take?

A standard residential door lock rekey typically takes 10-15 minutes per lock. For a whole-house rekey (5-6 locks), expect about 45-60 minutes total. Commercial rekeying may take longer depending on the number of locks and complexity of the master key system. We complete most residential service calls within an hour from arrival to completion.

Can you make car keys with transponder chips?

Yes, we program transponder keys, smart keys, key fobs, and proximity keys for all major vehicle makes and models. Our mobile units carry advanced key programming equipment to cut and program keys on-site. We can create new keys even if you've lost all existing keys, and our prices are typically 30-50% less than dealership rates with same-day service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, cash, Venmo, Zelle, and CashApp. For commercial clients, we also offer invoicing with net-30 payment terms for established accounts. Payment is due upon completion of service.

How long does it take to rekey a house?

Rekeying a standard residential lock takes approximately 10-15 minutes per lock. For an average home with 5-6 exterior locks, the complete rekeying service typically takes 60-90 minutes. We can rekey multiple locks to work with a single key for your convenience, making it easy to secure your entire home with one master key.

Do you offer senior or military discounts?

Yes! We proudly offer a 10% discount for active military, veterans, first responders, and seniors aged 65 and older on all non-emergency services. Please mention your eligibility when scheduling your appointment. Valid ID or proof of service is required at the time of service.

Can you install smart locks with my existing deadbolt?

Most smart locks are designed to replace your existing deadbolt completely. However, some models like the August Smart Lock can be installed over your existing deadbolt interior mechanism. During our free security consultation, we'll assess your current hardware and recommend the best smart lock solution that fits your door configuration, security needs, and budget.

What should I do if I'm locked out at night?

Call us immediately at (210) 619-3986. Our 24/7 emergency lockout service operates around the clock with no extra after-hours fees. Stay in a safe, well-lit area if possible. Our technician will arrive within 20-30 minutes on average and can unlock your door using non-destructive entry techniques. We'll also provide recommendations to prevent future lockouts.

How often should I replace my locks?

High-quality residential locks should be replaced every 7-10 years under normal use, or sooner if you notice difficulty turning the key, loose components, or visible wear. However, you should rekey or replace locks immediately after moving into a new home, losing keys, ending a relationship, or experiencing a break-in. Annual lock maintenance and lubrication can significantly extend lock lifespan.

What brands of locks do you install and service?

We install and service all major residential and commercial lock brands including Schlage, Kwikset, Yale, Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, Assa Abloy, August Smart Locks, Baldwin, Emtek, Von Duprin, Corbin Russwin, and more. We only recommend and install Grade 1 (highest security) and Grade 2 locks for our clients, avoiding lower-quality hardware that compromises your security.

Can you extract a broken key from my lock?

Yes, broken key extraction is one of our specialty services. Our technicians use professional extraction tools to carefully remove broken key fragments without damaging the lock cylinder. In most cases, we can extract the broken piece and cut a new key on-site so you can use the lock immediately. If the lock was damaged by the break, we can repair or replace it the same day.

Do you provide commercial access control systems?

These systems provide detailed audit trails, remote access management, scheduled access control, and integration with your existing security cameras and alarm systems. We serve businesses of all sizes from single-door offices to multi-building corporate campuses.
